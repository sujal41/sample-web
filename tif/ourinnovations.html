Centre for Social Research & Impacts (CSRi) is an initiative by TIF to
provide consulting services to impact organisations. Our services are
powered by knowledge and impact to achieve the equilibrium to our
work. We are driven by new-age thinking, and value aligned
networking and need based implementation, with community at the
core.
    We work with CSR foundations,NGOs, philanthropy initiatives,
social enterprises, and government departments by providing them
with hands-on support to plan, strategies, implement, and scale social
development programs.
